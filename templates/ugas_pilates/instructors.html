Ugana Maidarjav

Meet Ugana, a passionate and highly experienced Pilates instructor who brings a unique blend of artistic flair and meticulous discipline to her practice. Born in Khovd, Mongolia, Ugana began her journey as a contortionist, honing her skills from a young age. Her career as a performance artist took her across the globe, allowing her to share her talent and dedication with audiences worldwide.

With a background in hospitality and performance, Ugana has a deep appreciation for customer service and a keen eye for detail. She transitioned to yoga after the birth of her twin girls and completed her RYT200 training at Global Yoga Shala in France. Now, she combines her extensive knowledge of body movement and her passion for wellness to guide her clients towards a stronger core and improved flexibility through authentic Pilates practice.

Join Ugana at Uga's Pilates and experience the transformative power of her expertise and dedication. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, Ugana's classes will inspire and challenge you, helping you achieve your fitness goals and enhance your overall well-being.

Master Pilates Trainer Anna Rubau

We are delighted to introduce Anna, a Master Pilates Trainer whose expertise and dedication have profoundly shaped our Pilates community. Anna has been a guiding light for many, including our very own instructor, Ugana, who completed her True Pilates Teacher Training under Anna's exceptional mentorship.

With years of experience and a deep understanding of the Pilates method, Anna has earned a reputation as one of the foremost Pilates trainers in the field. Her approach combines precision, care, and a personalized touch, ensuring that each trainee reaches their full potential.

Though Anna is currently based in Spain, her influence continues to resonate here in Antwerp. For those interested in becoming a Pilates instructor, Ugana offers hands-on training to help you gain experience and hone your skills. Once you’re ready, you have the unique opportunity to complete your formal teacher training with Anna, benefiting from her extensive knowledge and mastery of the practice.

Whether you're taking your first steps into the world of Pilates or looking to deepen your practice, Anna’s legacy and expertise are available to guide you on your journey through our studio.

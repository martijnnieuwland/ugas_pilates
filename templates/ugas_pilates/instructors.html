Meet Your Instructor

Ugana Maidarjav

Meet Ugana, a passionate and highly experienced Pilates instructor who brings a unique blend of artistic flair and meticulous discipline to her practice. Born in Khovd, Mongolia, Ugana began her journey as a contortionist, honing her skills from a young age. Her career as a performance artist took her across the globe, allowing her to share her talent and dedication with audiences worldwide.

With a background in hospitality and performance, Ugana has a deep appreciation for customer service and a keen eye for detail. She transitioned to yoga after the birth of her twin girls and completed her RYT200 training at Global Yoga Shala in France. Now, she combines her extensive knowledge of body movement and her passion for wellness to guide her clients towards a stronger core and improved flexibility through authentic Pilates practice.

Join Ugana at Uga's Pilates and experience the transformative power of her expertise and dedication. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, Ugana's classes will inspire and challenge you, helping you achieve your fitness goals and enhance your overall well-being.

Anna Rubau

Anna Rubau is Spanish, originally from Gerona. She has been passionate about dance since her childhood; it is her first great love. While still young, she moves to Paris, where she dances in various projects, including the musical “Cats” and with Roland Petit. Through her hard work, extreme discipline, and perseverance, she achieves her dream of joining Maurice Béjart's ballet company.

At 22 years old, during a dress rehearsal, her partner mishandles a "jeté en l'air," causing her to fall and break her shoulder.

During her rehabilitation, she thinks, "This is the end of my artistic career." The director wanted her back on stage within four months, but she knows she will never be ready in time.

Welcome to Uga's Pilates Studio

Nestled in the tranquil surroundings of Brasschaat, just a stone's throw away from Antwerp, Uga's Pilates Studio offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere that we believe you'll love. Our studio is designed to be a sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of daily life, providing you with a perfect environment to focus on your wellness journey.

Our Mission

At Uga's Pilates Studio, our mission is to create a nurturing space where individuals can improve their physical health, mental well-being, and overall quality of life through the practice of Pilates. We are dedicated to helping you achieve your personal fitness goals in a supportive and welcoming atmosphere.

Why Choose Us

    Prime Location : Conveniently located in Brasschaat, our studio is easily accessible for those in Antwerp and the surrounding areas.
    Serene Atmosphere : Enjoy your Pilates sessions in a calm and peaceful environment that promotes relaxation and focus.
    Expert Instruction : Our highly trained instructors are passionate about Pilates and committed to guiding you through safe and effective workouts.
